Refreshing Break

Baguio City, the "Summer Capital of the Philippines" is a popular place because of the many tourism it has like Burnham and SM. However, the recent number of visitors brought many challenges for the city and its citizens. Although the City is benefiting from the increase of tourists by also increasing the demand of hotels, restaurants, souvenir shops, etc, it is also a challenge for the citizens of Baguio.

Baguio City is experiencing a significant influx of tourists, particularly during peak seasons and long weekends. This influx has led to a number of issues, like traffic congestion, environmental strain, overcrowding and impact on local culture.

The data on tourist arrivals shows that the city government has seen a significant increase in the number of tourists visiting Baguio City in recent years. Traffic Congestion has become a major problem in the city particularly in peak seasons. Surveys have shown that both citizens and businesses are concerned about the impact of tourism in the City. This led to citizens adjusting and staying home.

The influx of tourists in Baguio City has both positive and negative sides, it gives opportunities and challenges. While it provides economic benefits, it also threatens the city's natural beauty and cultural integrity.

By working together, the city government, tourism stakeholders, and residents can ensure that Baguio City remains a vibrant and sustainable destination for years to come. If you are a tourist, one of the best ways to lower the environmental impact or tourism is to travel places outside of their busiest season.

- Elisha Rodrigo
